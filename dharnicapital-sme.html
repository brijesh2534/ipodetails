Company Details

Dharni Capital Services Limited is engaged in a variety of financial services providing businesses. The company offer diversified financial services such as: Mutual Fund Distribution Services Fixed Deposit Distribution Services Real Estate Brokerage Services Technical Consultancy and Outsourcing

The Company holds AMFI Registration number ARN-144504. Dharni Capital Services is an empaneled distributor with SBI Mutual Fund, HDFC Mutual Fund, ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund, Aditya Birla Sunlife Mutual Fund, Nippon Mutual Fund, Kotak Mutual Fund, UTI Mutual Fund, Axis Mutual Fund and many more.

Dharni Capital Services is also registered with Karnataka RERA Authority as an Agent and its registration number is PRM/KA/RERA/1251/446/AG/210813/002502. The company is registered as a Real Estate Broker with large players like Prestige Estate Projects and Embassy Group.

Dharni Capital Services is also empaneled as a Fixed Deposit Broker with pioneers like Shriram Transport Finance Limited, HDFC Limited, ICICI Home Finance Limited and PNB Housing Finance Limited.
